About

Since 1993, Neopac srl, led by founder Claudio Crucitti, has been designing and manufacturing micro-dosing, dosing, counting and vertical packaging systems. While many manufacturers focused on speed races, Neopac machines focused on something else: maximum precision, robustness, reliability and long-lasting performance. That is our focus. All machines are engineered to be tough and durable for intensive shifts, serving customers who need to minimize downtime or integrate the packager into properly sized production lines, aiming for high productivity over time at low cost rather than record speed. • Ease of use • Custom-designed components that make the machines truly unique • Qualified installation and technical support • Spare parts stock always available • Tailor-made solutions for every production need Our mission is to provide not just a machine, but a reliable partner that supports the growth and efficiency of our customers' production.
